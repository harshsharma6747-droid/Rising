Academics

We offer a comprehensive curriculum from Nursery to 10th Standard, following CBSE guidelines. Our focus is on holistic development, critical thinking, and a love for lifelong learning.

Pre-Primary (Nursery, LKG, UKG)

Focus on play-based learning, motor skills, and social development in a colorful and nurturing environment.

Primary (Classes 1-5)

Building strong foundations in literacy, numeracy, EVS, and computer science through interactive lessons.

Middle (Classes 6-8)

Developing critical thinking, project-based learning, and advanced concepts in Science, Maths, and Social Studies.

Secondary (Classes 9-10)

Preparing students for board examinations with a rigorous academic program and dedicated support.
